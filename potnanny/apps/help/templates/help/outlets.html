Outlets

Intro

Pot Nanny uses a 433Mhz wireless transmitter to turn remote power outlets on or off.

Up to 254 wireless power outlets can be, theoretically, controlled by the system.

Adding an outlet to the system

  1. Navigate to the Outlet page.
  2. Click on the ADD outlet button that looks like a + symbol.
  3. Give the new Outlet a name. It must be unique from other Outlet names.
  4. Assign the Outlet a channel. It must also be unique.
  5. Click Submit.

Pairing the wireless outlet with the application

  1. Plug a wireless outlet into the wall.
  2. Hold down the learning button on the outlet for 5 seconds, until the light begins to blink.
  3. On the main Outlet page of the application, click the on/off button for the new outlet to "on".
  4. The light on the remote outlet should begin to blink faster.
  5. On the Outlet page, click the on/off button back to "off".
The outlet is now paired to the application!

Testing the outlet

  1. Plug an appliance (like, a light) into the new wireless outlet.
  2. On the main Outlet page of the application, click the on/off button for the new outlet to "on". The appliance should turn on.

Pairing multiple outlets to the same channel

  1. Plug in two, or more, outlets, and set them both into learning mode.
  2. Click the on/off button to "on" to pair the two outlets to the same channel.
  3. On the Outlet page, click the on/off button back to "off".

Setting up outlet schedules

  1. From the main Outlet page, click on an outlet name to enter edit mode for the outlet..
  2. In the Outlet Schedules section, click the "add" button (+) to add a Schedule.
  3. There are three Schedule buttons:
    • 12 hour ON This is for the flowering light cycle.
    • 18 hour ON The is for the vegatative growing cycle.
    • Custom You decide the on/off times, and what days the schedule will run.
